PinFlow

A personal, local workflow for preparing selected fashion and lifestyle images and publishing them to boards owned by the operator.

What the application does

  • Searches Pixabay or Pexels photos and prepares visual previews.
  • Adds user-supplied text to selected, licensed images.
  • Lists boards belonging to the authorized Pinterest account.
  • Creates a Pin only after the operator reviews and confirms it.
  • Saves a specific public Pin through the official Save Pin API.

The application does not scrape Pinterest, collect account passwords, automate engagement, or publish without explicit confirmation.

Data and attribution

API credentials remain on the operator's local device. Pinterest account data is requested only when needed for the selected operation and is not sold or shared.

Images are sourced from Pixabay or Pexels. Photographer and source attribution are retained whenever applicable.
